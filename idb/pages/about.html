About

In this website an internet database is created that follows a similar format as IMDb. The database has 3 kinds of pages or subjects that form the database. All 3 pages or subjects are connected and each item in the database belongs to 1 type of page or subject and has links to items in the other 2 subjects. The database for this group will be a Pokémon database. The 3 kinds of pages or subjects will be Pokémon, Pokémon Type, and Evolution Chain.
